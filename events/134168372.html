About the World Startup Report & Bowei Gai 


Bowei Gai is a serial entrepreneur from Silicon Valley who sold the company he co-founded, CardMunch, to LinkedIn in 2011. On New Year's Eve of 2013, he boarded his first flight for a 9-months long trip across 29 countries and 36 cities to research the world's startup ecosystems.


Its purpose is to:


• Document the world's startups ecosystems: culture, stories, strengths and challenges.

• Share the learnings with a global audience and tell the story of each local startup ecosystem.

• Connect the local entrepreneurs and active members of the startup community to others like them from around the world.
